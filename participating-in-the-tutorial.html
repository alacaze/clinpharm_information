7.5 Participating in the tutorial

7.5.1 Raise Hand

You will be asked at times to raise your hand if you have any questions or if the coordinator is doing an activity. This is found by clicking on the ‘manage participants’ icon at the bottom of the screen. The ‘raise hand’ option is available at the bottom of this section.

7.5.2 Chat

If you have a question or a comment, you can post it in the chat option. Click on the ‘chat’ icon at the bottom of the screen and it will be moved to the right hand side.

7.5.3 Presenting in the tutorial

You should join the tutorial at least 15 minutes before the commencement of the tutorial. This will give you time to test your microphone and speakers. If you would like to verbally check your audio with someone else, the other students will be able to assist if you can be heard clearly.
